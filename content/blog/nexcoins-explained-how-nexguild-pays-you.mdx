Most "earn money online" platforms are vague about the actual math. I built NexGuild, so I'll just tell you exactly how it works — the real exchange rate, the real commission split, and the real redemption path.

## The exchange rate

NexCoins are NexGuild's internal currency:

- **1,000 NexCoins = 1 USD**
- **1 INR ≈ 12.5 NexCoins**
- Daily earning cap: **15,000 NexCoins per contributor** (about $15/day at full utilization)

Every coin you earn comes from a real source: completing a task, finishing a survey through an offerwall like CPX Research or TheoremReach, or referral commission through the NexLeader program. There's no "watch this ad to unlock a mystery reward" mechanic — every credit traces back to a specific completed action.

## Where the money actually comes from

When you complete a paid task or a survey, the gross payout runs through a commission split:

- **66% to you**, the contributor who did the work
- **10% to your NexLeader** — your referrer or guild leader, paid automatically for as long as you're active
- **24% to the platform**, which funds moderation, payment processing, and voucher fulfillment

This split happens through a single function every time — `creditWithCommission()` — so there's no manual crediting and no inconsistency between how different task types pay out.

## Eight ways to prove you did the work

NexGuild supports eight independent task-verification types, and admins can mix them freely on a single task:

| Type | How it's verified |
|---|---|
| Text / File submission | You type a response or upload a file |
| Proof Code | An 8-character code generated by a widget on the destination site |
| Telegram Join / Bot | Verified automatically via Telegram's Bot API |
| YouTube / X follow, retweet, like | Screenshot upload, reviewed manually |

This matters because it's the difference between a platform that can actually stop fraud and one that just hopes people are honest.

## Turning coins into real money

Coins redeem for vouchers — Amazon, Flipkart, Paytm, PhonePe, Swiggy, Zomato — delivered by email within 48 hours. There's also the **NexLeader** system: if you build a guild of contributors under your referral link, you earn 10% of everything they earn, indefinitely. That's the closest thing to passive income the platform offers, and it's fully transparent in the ledger.
